Experienced engineers. Direct relationships.

We provide practical, cost-effective civil engineering services for public and private clients. As a local, streamlined firm, we offer direct access to experienced engineers, responsive communication, and solutions shaped around each client's priorities.

From new infrastructure to the rehabilitation of aging facilities, our work is technically sound, buildable, economical, and attentive to the realities of island communities and public works budgets.

Engineering services

Practical solutions from planning through construction.

Thoughtful engineering for essential facilities and infrastructure, delivered with clear communication and careful attention to long-term value.

01

Water Systems

Engineering for municipal and private water systems, including source development, distribution, storage, pump stations, pressure improvements, hydraulic analysis, regulatory compliance, and system rehabilitation.

02

Wastewater Systems

Collection and treatment solutions for public and private clients, including gravity sewers, pump stations, force mains, septic support, rehabilitation, permitting, and treatment-facility improvements.

03

Stormwater Management

Drainage studies, stormwater plans, detention and retention facilities, conveyance, erosion control, and low-impact development designed for regulatory compliance and practical maintenance.

04

Structural Engineering

Structural analysis and design for buildings and civil infrastructure, including foundations, retaining walls, equipment foundations, small bridges, rehabilitation, code review, and construction support.

05

Pavement Engineering

Evaluation, rehabilitation, and new pavement design for roads, parking areas, airports, industrial facilities, and utility projects, with grading, drainage, and construction observation coordinated for lasting performance.
